Krabby Patty

The Krabby Patty is THE culinary delight of the Bikini Bottom. Adored by many sea-dwellers, one needs only a bite to see what all the sensation around this dish is all about. It is also reportedly, frequently seen stolen by the establishment's tiny arch-nemesis.

The ingredients you will need are:
  • Steamed Hamburger Buns
  • A Krabby Patty
  • A Teaspoon of Ketchup
  • A Teaspoon of Mustard
  • Three Sea Pickles
  • Two Slices of Onions
  • Lettuce
  • A Slice of Tomato
  • A Slice of Cheese

The recipe is as follows:
  1. Bun
  2. Patty
  3. Ketchup
  4. Mustard
  5. Pickles
  6. Extra Onions
  7. Lettuce
  8. Tomatoes
  9. Cheese
  10. Bun

Note: This is the 'Crying Johnny', which must specifically be assembled in that order
